About Us!

We are committed to preserve the beauty of our planet

For over two decades, we've been on a mission to give our carbon footprint a serious makeover. We've turbocharged the energy efficiency of all our spots, jazzed up the use of homegrown green power, and aced our no-offset challenges at our sites. Our renewable energy game plan is like a world tour - tailored to each country's unique vibes. We've mainly hitched a ride on locally-sourced renewables or cranked up our own clean energy shindigs, featuring biomass, biomethanization, solar panels, and more!

For the Planet

As global warming ushers in environmental shifts that pose a lasting threat to both human and natural habitats, including rising sea levels, glacier melt, ocean warming, acidification, and a surge in extreme weather events, the urgency has never been greater. In response, L'Oréal is elevating its efforts with a bold initiative named "L'Oréal for the Future." Our commitments for 2030 mark the outset of a more profound transformation and exemplify our vision of what a company's mission, purpose, and responsibilities ought to be in confronting the world's pressing challenges.

Innovating through Science

More than 15 years ago, the realm of Green Sciences unfurled a remarkable avenue for innovation, allowing L'Oréal to embark on a sustainable makeover of its arsenal of raw materials, actives, and formulations. Nature and the vibrant world of living organisms stand as a boundless wellspring of scientific breakthroughs and an endless well of inspiration for crafting the beauty that ignites the world.

Innovating for people

L'Oréal's dedicated teams have ramped up their efforts to furnish consumers with comprehensive insights into all product ingredients. We're sharing why these components are included in cosmetic products, their effective concentrations, their origins, and their traceability. Furthermore, we're committed to sourcing them with the utmost care for the environment and considering the ethical and social aspects of their production. It's all about transparency and empowerment for our customers.
